Matching Questions

“Is your nearest ____ the same as my ____?”

Answer: Yes / No • 5 min • Draw 3 keep 1
Transit
Transit Line (must be moving, Yes if seeker's train would stop at hider's station), Station Name Length (count spaces & hyphens, include “station” if shown in mapping app), Street/Path.
Administrative Divisions
Naselja, Četrtne skupnosti from rulebook.
Natural
Park.
Places of Interest
Museum, Movie Theater, Library, Embassy, Police Station.

Measuring Questions

“Compared to me, are you closer to or further from ____?”

Answer: Closer / Further • Include their distance • Draw 3 keep 1
Transit
Rail Stations (Polje, Zalog, Vodmat, Črnuče, Ljubljana, Trzin, Brinje, Ježica, Rakovnik).
Borders
Distance to Četrtne skupnosti borders from rulebook.
Natural
Sea Level, Body of Water, Park, Forest.
Places of Interest
Museum, Movie Theater.
Rules Clarifications
If the feature does not exist within map boundaries, answer is null.

Radar Questions

Answer: Yes / No • Draw 2 keep 1
Distance Options
400m, 800m, 2km, 5km, 8km, 16km, 40km, 80km, 160km, or custom.
Rules Clarifications
Radar checks your exact current position, not your hiding zone.
