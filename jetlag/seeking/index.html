Hide & Seek – Quick Rules

Matching Questions

“Is your nearest ____ the same as my ____?”

Answer: Yes / No • 5 min • Draw 3 keep 1
Transit
Transit Line (must be moving). Yes only if your train would stop at their station. Station Name Length (count spaces & hyphens, include “station” if shown). Street/Path ends when name changes.
Administrative Divisions
Naselja and Četrtne skupnosti must match exactly from the rules.
Natural
Park = Google Maps tree icon. Measure distance from the map icon.
Places of Interest
Museum, Movie Theater, Library, Embassy, Police Station. Must match Google Maps categorization.

Measuring Questions

“Compared to me, are you closer to or further from ____?”

Answer: Closer / Further • Include their distance • Draw 3 keep 1
Transit
Rail Stations: Polje, Zalog, Vodmat, Črnuče, Ljubljana, Trzin, Brinje, Ježica, Rakovnik.
Borders
Distance to Četrtne skupnosti borders based on official map.
Natural
Sea Level (altitude). Body of Water (named, not pools). Park (Google Maps icon). Forest (official map).
Places of Interest
Museum and Movie Theater categorized by Google Maps.
Null Rule
If the feature does not exist within map boundaries, answer is null.

Radar Questions

“Are you within ____ of me?”

Answer: Yes / No • Draw 2 keep 1
Distance Options
400m, 800m, 2km, 5km, 8km, 16km, 40km, 80km, 160km, or custom.
Current Location Rule
Radar checks your exact current position, not your hiding zone.

Thermometer Questions

“After traveling ____, am I hotter or colder?”

Answer: Hotter / Colder • Draw 2 keep 1
Sizes
800m, 5km, 16km, 80km.
How It Works
If seeker’s new location is closer to you → Hotter. Otherwise → Colder.

Photo Questions

10 min • Valid photo or null • Draw 1 keep 1
Small Game
Building from Transit Station, Widest Street, Tree, Tallest Structure, You (Selfie), Sky, Trace 800m Street, 2 Buildings.
Medium Game
Tallest Building from Transit Station, Trace Nearest Street, Restaurant Interior, Park, Grocery Aisle, Place of Worship, Train Platform.
Photo Rules
No zoom. Default lens. Must meet full framing requirements. No Street View or intentional image searching.
