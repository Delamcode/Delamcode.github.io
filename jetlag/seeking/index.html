Hide & Seek – Game Rules

Matching Questions

Format: “Is your nearest ____ the same as my ____?”

Answer: Yes / No

Time: 5 minutes

After answering: Draw 3 cards, keep 1.

Measuring Questions

Format: “Compared to me, are you closer to or further from ____?”

Answer: Closer / Further

Must include: Their distance

After answering: Draw 3 cards, keep 1.

Radar Questions

Format: “Are you within ____ of me?”

Distances: 400m, 800m, 2km, 5km, 8km, 16km, 40km, 80km, 160km, or custom

Answer: Yes / No

After answering: Draw 2 cards, keep 1.

Thermometer Questions

Format: “After traveling ____, am I hotter or colder?”

Sizes: 800m, 5km, 16km, 80km

Answer: Hotter / Colder

After answering: Draw 2 cards, keep 1.

Photo Questions

Answer: Valid photo OR null

Time: 10 minutes (small & medium)

After answering: Draw 1 card, keep 1.

Key Rules

  • Use Google Maps classification.
  • Measure distance from map icons.
  • If outside map boundaries → treat as nonexistent.
  • Seekers must clarify ambiguous questions.
  • GPS may not be perfectly accurate.
